WHAT is School of Styles

(Classes and Workshops)

School Of Styles is a place to gain great training knowledge about dance and the dance industry itself. A place that you can learn multiple styles such as Hip Hop, Popping, House, Grooves, dancing to Afro Beat music and freestyle.

Pete has been an educator of dance for many years now. Not only does he show you the steps. He teaches by breaking down movement so that you can fully understand how to move with your full range, full execution and precision.

School of Styles is a place that caters for:

    • 1 on 1 personal training (Online) or (In Studio)
      Group training sessions (Online) or (In Studio)

    • Outside of these sessions, Pete frequently teaches online dance Intensives. These last throughout the week for both advanced and beginners. For inquiries send a message via the contact page.

    Pete has an outstanding ability to teach dancers and non-dancers how to move. Whether it be movement coaching for professionals and taking their career to the next level. Coaching actors, artists, teaching online groups or In House classes to a few of the Worlds Best Global Brands. Pete does it all from choreographing Gwen Stefani's, "Misery" video to training Travis Barker's kids how to dance. The list goes on.
